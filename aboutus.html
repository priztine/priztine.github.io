| How It Started |

Socialoft started out as a small idea from a 25 year old kid that had an inspiration for men's fashion. He wanted to create something unique from other men's clothing brand to help people discover their own style. So he started small with creating a blog where he gained a lot of traction and support by fellow members. Which gave him an idea to start his own store and see where it will go. Alive came "Socialoft" a unique and extraordinary store for men's fashion. Since then he has opened two locations one in Miami and another in California which have been a huge hit. He has now opted in the Internet scene and decided to create a webstore like no other. Letting men around the world shop from the comfort of their home.
